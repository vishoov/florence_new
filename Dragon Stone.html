Dragon Stone

Bold, Nature-Inspired Designer Stone
Dragon Stone Granite is a bold, nature-inspired designer stone with fluid wave-like veining in deep charcoals and soft greys, evoking the raw energy of ancient rock formations. Its dynamic movement and organic texture make it a standout material for luxury spaces seeking depth, character, and durability.

Finish & Features

  • Textured & Leather Finish: Enhances the natural grain of the stone, providing a sensory matte surface with a subtle sheen.
  • Extreme Durability: Highly resistant to scratches, heat, stains, and weathering—perfect for high-traffic areas and outdoor use.
  • Custom Thickness: Offered in 2cm to 3cm thickness for customized design versatility.
  • Natural Flowing Aesthetic: The undulating stone formations create an artistic and sculptural effect, perfect for modern architecture.

Ideal Applications

  • Exterior Facades & Wall Claddings: Adds depth and visual intrigue to any structure.
  • Luxury Interiors: Ideal for feature walls, statement countertops, and bespoke furniture pieces.
  • High-Traffic Flooring & Pavements: Slip-resistant and engineered for long-lasting durability.
  • Commercial & Hospitality Spaces: Perfect for upscale hotels, offices, and design-centric spaces.
