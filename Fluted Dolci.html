Fluted Dolci

Fluted Dolci: A Symphony of Elegance & Texture
Fluted Dolci is a premium designer granite, crafted to elevate architectural spaces with its deeply grooved, fluted texture and refined natural veining. The interplay of soft greys and intricate dark veining creates a visually dynamic surface, making it a perfect statement piece for contemporary and classical settings. Fluted Dolci transforms stone into an artistic expression, blending textures, movement and timeless elegance.

Finish & Features

  • Fluted Surface: Grooves carved with precision to add depth, rhythm and sculptural beauty.
  • Natural Marble-Like Veining: The organic movement of dark veins against a neutral backdrop enhances elegance.
  • Durability & Strength: Highly resistant to scratches, heat, stains, and weathering‚Äîperfect for both indoor and outdoor areas.
  • Custom Thickness: Available in 1cm to 2cm, tailored for bespoke designs.

Ideal Applications

  • Exterior Facades & Claddings: Adds a sculptural and architectural element to modern structures
  • Luxury Interiors: Ideal for accent walls, feature panels and designer furniture
  • High-Traffic Flooring & Pavements: Provides durability with a sophisticated aesthetic
  • Commercial & Hospitality Spaces: A refined choice for upscale hotels, offices and retail interiors
